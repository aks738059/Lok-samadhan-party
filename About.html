The Lok samadhan Party’s entry into politics in 2025 made it one of the thousands of recognised, registered parties in India. It was on 30st August, 2024 that the party was allotted an election symbol by the Election Commission. After submitting the ‘broom’, ‘candle’ and ‘tap’ as its most preferred symbols from the list of free symbols, the party was granted its first preference of the ‘broom’. A statement issued by the party on that day said, “With this, the party has crossed the second milestone (after party’s registration) on its way to cleaning up the polity of the country. With the broom, which symbolises dignity of labour, the party hopes to clean the filth which has permeated our government and our legislature. The country needs a clean sweep of its corrupted mainstream political parties.
